✦ Our Story

Katy's Not Just a
Groomer — She's Family

Nestled inside Balcones Animal Hospital on McNeil Drive, Katy's Grooming has been a beloved Austin institution for over a decade. What started as one woman's passion for animals has blossomed into Northwest Austin's most trusted grooming studio.

Katy has an incredible gift with animals — even the most anxious dogs and skittish cats leave relaxed, fluffy, and beaming. Her regulars drive across Austin because nobody does it quite like Katy.
